# Offer Insights — Final with Economic Reframing

Plain-English observations that change mental models where the data supports it. Strategy-outcome template with reframing layered in: not just what happened, but what it reveals about the buyer or the funnel economics.

## GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) — Sales Page

### Insights

- **Pricing GlowSerum Pro × 1 at $47 instead of $45 decreased take rate by 0.4%** (3.1% vs. 3.5%). The $2 increase crossed a price-sensitivity threshold where meaningfully fewer single-unit buyers converted — at current traffic, the margin gained on the higher price doesn't offset the volume lost.

## GlowSerum Pro × 2 ($94.00) — Sales Page

### Insights

- **Pricing GlowSerum Pro × 2 at $94 instead of $90 decreased take rate by 0.3%** (4.1% vs. 4.4%). The $4 increase looks small on paper but crosses a price threshold — the $90 version produced $37k more in revenue at the same view volume.

- **Customers who chose GlowSerum Pro × 2 at $94 spent an average of $105** — $11 of additional items at checkout. The attach beyond the bundle itself is where the real margin sits; the bundle price anchors the order, but the downstream add-ons compound it.
