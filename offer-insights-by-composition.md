# Offer Insights by Offer Composition

## GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00)

| Metric | Value |
|---|---|
| Page Type | Sales Page |
| Sell Price | $47.00 |
| Shopify Price | $49.00 |
| Discount | 4.1% |
| Views | 22,100 |
| Orders | 695 |
| Take Rate | 3.1% |
| AOV | $51.70 |
| Revenue | $32,665 |
| Configurations | 3 |

### Insights

**GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) take rate rises 12% when positioned as the middle option in a 3-tier ladder**

*Middle-position effect: GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) converts better as the 2nd of 3 choices*

- When shown between a lower-priced anchor and a higher-priced value tier, GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) captures the largest share of orders
- The effect is consistent across traffic sources — not driven by a single ad or audience
- Velocity has tested GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) in 4 different page configurations; the 3-tier ladder outperforms 2-offer pages by 12%

**Pairing GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) with a bundle 3rd tier lifts its revenue per customer by $8 vs. plain 3-pack**

*Bundle as value tier increases GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) downstream attach*

- Customers who chose GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) on pages with a bundle 3rd tier added more at checkout
- Average checkout attach was $14.20 vs. $6.40 on pages with a plain 3-pack as the value tier
- Hypothesis: the bundle anchors a higher perceived order value, making checkout add-ons feel smaller

**GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) underperforms on pages where the anchor is priced within $5 — price gap matters**

*Narrow price gap to anchor reduces GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) take rate*

- When the ×1 anchor is $47 and GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) is $94 (2x gap), take rate is 4.1%
- When the ×1 anchor is $45 and GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) is $90 (2x gap), take rate is 4.3%
- The absolute dollar gap matters less than the ratio — Velocity is testing wider gaps
