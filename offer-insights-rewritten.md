## GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) — Sales Page

**Metrics:** 22,100 views · 695 orders · 3.1% take rate · $51.70 AOV · $32,665 revenue · 3 configurations

### Insights

- **GlowSerum Pro × 1 ($47.00) held a 3.1% take rate when positioned as the entry-tier anchor** across 3 configurations, with the highest performance (3.4%) appearing when paired with GlowSerum Pro × 2 at $94 and GlowSerum Pro × 3 at $141. The 2x price step to the target tier produced the cleanest anchor dynamic.

- **GlowSerum Pro × 1 at $47.00 underperformed the $45.00 version by 0.4% in take rate** (3.1% vs. 3.5%) despite only a $2 price difference, suggesting the $47 threshold sits above a meaningful price-sensitivity boundary for the single-unit configuration.
